ANIM CHOPPIFY

HOW TO USE:
1. Paste your Export Code into the box below.
2. Ensure you have a Skill Container named "Choppy".
3. Inside that container, have an Anim Node with your target animation and preview time.
4. Choose your FPS or a specific interval and hit "CHOPPIFY". The tool will slice the animation into static steps.
